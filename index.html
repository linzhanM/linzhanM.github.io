About Me

I am a final-year undergraduate student from Chu Kochen Honors College at Zhejiang University with an overall GPA of 3.98/4.0, ranking top 2%. I work closely with Prof. Xiaowei Zhou, Prof. Sida Peng at the ZJU3DV Lab and Prof. Yuxiong Wang at the UIUC Vision Group. My research interest lies in the field of 3D computer vision, particularly including 3D reconstruction and 3D generation, as well as their applications in robotics and autonomous driving.
